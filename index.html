Common Questions

Start with clear information.

Does submitting this form mean I am approved?

No. A consultation request is not a loan application, approval, preapproval, offer or commitment to lend.

What factors can affect mortgage qualification?

Depending on the program, lenders may review credit, income, employment history, assets, debt-to-income ratio, down payment, property type and other factors.

Is the calculator my final monthly payment?

No. It estimates principal and interest only. Actual housing costs may also include property taxes, homeowners insurance, mortgage insurance, HOA dues and other expenses.

Where should I submit sensitive financial information?

Only through the secure mortgage application or other approved secure systems—not through the general consultation form.
